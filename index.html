Thành Phố Hồ Chí Minh

Monday 15 August 2024

Dubbed the "Pearl of the Far East," Ho Chi Minh City has long been the cultural, economic, and political center of Vietnam. It is a convergence of many cultures, offering diverse tourism products. Known as the "city that never sleeps," it boasts lively entertainment activities both day and night. When traveling to Ho Chi Minh City, you will encounter high-rise buildings standing close together, bustling entertainment venues, and busy shopping centers. However, the city also retains old villas and traditional markets that have existed for hundreds of years.

Price: $100

Hà Nội

Saturday 19 August 2024

Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is renowned for its centuries-old architecture and rich culture with influences from Southeast Asia, China, and France. The city center is the bustling Old Quarter, where narrow streets are named after their original trades. There are many small temples, including Bach Ma, which honors a legendary horse, as well as Dong Xuan Market, which sells household goods and street food.

Price: $120

Đà Lạt

Friday 23 August 2024

Dubbed a summer paradise during the sweltering days, Da Lat is a city on the Langbiang plateau that remains cool and clear all year round. Known as the city of mist and flowers, and the city of thousands of pines whispering in the wind, Da Lat is a picturesque and poetic destination. Many people want to travel to Da Lat not only for these reasons but also because of the winding roads on the hills, the city's lack of traffic lights, and the friendly, hospitable people. It would be a pity not to visit Da Lat at least once in a lifetime.

Price: $90

Nha Trang

Monday 3 September 2024

Nha Trang is a coastal city and the political, economic, cultural, scientific, technical, and tourism center of Khánh Hòa Province. Currently, many Cham relics still exist, as this land once belonged to the Kingdom of Champa. It is also recognized as one of the most beautiful bays in the world, with a tranquil city located right by the sea. Traveling to Nha Trang, you will have the opportunity to explore the cuisine and places of a land known as the "land of Agarwood, the sea of Salanganes' nests."

Price: $110

Huế

Thursday 6 September 2024

Located in central Vietnam, Hue is a city recognized as a World Cultural Heritage site. The ancient capital boasts a long history and tradition with unique values and distinct identity.

Price: $140

Đà Nẵng

Sunday 9 September 2024

Da Nang is the city of bridges and is known as the most livable city in Vietnam. Famous for the image of the Dragon Bridge, both strong and majestic, Da Nang is blessed by nature with clear blue beaches and a series of impressive islands. Da Nang tourism is increasingly developing, attracting a large number of domestic and international tourists. The vast My Khe Beach, next to the magnificent Marble Mountains and the "precious gem" of Son Tra Peninsula, all these scenic spots contribute to providing a true experience for visitors.

Price: $160

Quảng Ninh

Tuesday 11 September 2024

Quảng Ninh, located in the northeastern region of our country, is captivating with its stunning beauty of mountains, rivers, and islands. Most prominent is Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO-recognized World Natural Heritage site. Visiting Ha Long, tourists will have the opportunity to admire the breathtaking scenery formed by 1,900 different limestone islands and many mysterious caves.

Price: $100

Tây Ninh

Saturday 15 September 2024

Tay Ninh tourism is blessed by nature with mountains, forests, and beautiful lakes. Located not too far from Saigon, this is indeed an ideal travel destination for those with limited vacation time.

Price: $100
