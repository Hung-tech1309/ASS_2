Thành Phố Hồ Chí Minh

Monday 15 August 2024

Dubbed the "Pearl of the Far East," Ho Chi Minh City has long been the cultural, economic, and political center of Vietnam. It is a convergence of many cultures, offering diverse tourism products. Known as the "city that never sleeps," it boasts lively entertainment activities both day and night. When traveling to Ho Chi Minh City, you will encounter high-rise buildings standing close together, bustling entertainment venues, and busy shopping centers. However, the city also retains old villas and traditional markets that have existed for hundreds of years.

Price: $100

Hà Nội

Saturday 19 August 2024

Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is renowned for its centuries-old architecture and rich culture with influences from Southeast Asia, China, and France. The city center is the bustling Old Quarter, where narrow streets are named after their original trades. There are many small temples, including Bach Ma, which honors a legendary horse, as well as Dong Xuan Market, which sells household goods and street food.

Price: $120

Đà Lạt

Friday 23 August 2024

Dubbed a summer paradise during the sweltering days, Da Lat is a city in the central highlands known for its cool climate, pine forests, and beautiful flowers. It's a popular tourist destination, offering attractions like Xuan Huong Lake, Dalat Flower Gardens, and the Valley of Love.

Price: $110
